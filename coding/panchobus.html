Project Overview

Panchobus is a campus transport app that allows students to check bus routes and reserve seats. The platform simplifies mobility by offering a clear, intuitive, and efficient experience.

HTML5 CSS JavaScript Responsive Design

Problem and Solution

University transportation can often feel disorganized and inefficient. Students may struggle to:

  • Understand available routes
  • Know schedules and stops
  • Secure a seat on time

This creates uncertainty and a poor user experience.

Panchobus simplifies the system through a user-centered platform where students can:

  • View routes visually on a map
  • Check stops and schedules
  • Reserve seats digitally
  • Access reservations through QR codes

The goal was to create a clear, efficient, and reliable experience.
